Privacy

StableWeather Privacy Policy

This release-preparation notice will be replaced by the final policy before StableWeather is publicly released.

Local data

Horse profiles, rug inventories, stable details, recommendation history, and preferences are stored locally on the user's device. StableWeather has no accounts, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Weather services

Stable addresses may be sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim for geocoding. Stable coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather forecasts. Horse profiles and rug inventories are not included in these requests.

Backups

Backup export and import are initiated by the user through their device's file or sharing controls.
