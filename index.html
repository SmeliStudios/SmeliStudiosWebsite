About SmeliStudios

SmeliStudios is a small independent software studio founded by horse owners who wanted simpler, more practical tools for everyday stable life.

After years of managing feed plans, checking weather forecasts, planning turnout, and keeping track of horse care, we found ourselves repeatedly wishing for software that was focused, affordable, easy to use and easy to share.

We're not building large management platforms or subscription ecosystems. We believe horse owners should be able to afford useful tools, use them for years, and spend their time thinking about their horses instead of their software.

Our goal is simple: build tools that make life easier for horse owners, one stable day at a time.
